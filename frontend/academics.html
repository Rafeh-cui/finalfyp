Attendance Policy

Minimum 75% attendance is required in every course. Falling below this threshold bars you from sitting in the final exam. Medical leaves require documentation submitted within 48 hours.

Credit Hours

  • Standard load: 15–18 credit hours per semester
  • Maximum (Dean's permission): 21 credit hours
  • Minimum to stay enrolled: 9 credit hours

Academic Standing

  • Good Standing: CGPA ≥ 2.0
  • Probation: CGPA 1.5 – 1.99 for one semester
  • Dismissal risk: CGPA below 1.5 consecutively
  • Dean's List: Semester GPA ≥ 3.5 with full load

FLEX Portal — Student Self Service

  • ✅ Course registration & add/drop

Registration Timeline

  • Registration opens: 2 weeks before semester start
  • Add/Drop period: First 2 weeks of semester
  • Course withdrawal (W grade): Up to week 8
  • Late registration penalty: Rs 500 per course/day

Repeater / Improvement Policy

  • F grade courses must be repeated in the next offered semester
  • D grade courses can be improved once for better GPA
  • Repeater students attend regular classes with batch
  • Max 2 repetitions of a failed course allowed

⏱️ Exam Season Survival Guide

📅 Plan Ahead
Break your syllabus into daily goals 2 weeks before exams. Use a planner or app.
🍅 Pomodoro Technique
Study 25 minutes, break 5 minutes. Every 4 rounds take a 20-minute break.
🧘 Manage Anxiety
4-7-8 breathing: inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s. Repeat 3 times before exam.
💤 Sleep is Non-Negotiable
7–8 hours of sleep improves memory consolidation. All-nighters reduce performance by 40%.

🌿 Daily Wellness Checklist

💧 8 glasses of water
🚶 30-min walk / exercise
📵 1-hr phone-free study block
🥗 Balanced meal, no skipping
📓 Journal 5 min before bed
👥 Connect with a friend

🔬 Graduate Programs

MS Programs at Attock Campus

🖥️ MS Computer Science 30 Cr.Hr
⚙️ MS Software Engineering 30 Cr.Hr
⚡ MS Electrical Engineering 30 Cr.Hr
💼 MBA (Business Administration) 36 Cr.Hr

Program Structure (MS)

  • Duration: 1.5 – 2 years (3–4 semesters)
  • Coursework: 24 credit hours minimum
  • Thesis / Research Project: 6 credit hours
  • Comprehensive exam before thesis defense

Academic Requirements

  • BS / BCS / BE degree (16 years education)
  • Minimum CGPA: 2.5 / 4.0
  • Relevant background in discipline
  • Deficiency courses may be required for cross-discipline applicants

Test Requirements

  • Valid NTS-GAT (General) score — minimum 50 percentile
  • OR valid GRE General score (accepted)
  • COMSATS internal test also accepted in some cycles

Application Documents

  • BS transcripts (all semesters, attested)
  • Statement of Purpose (1–2 pages)
  • Two recommendation letters from faculty supervisors
  • CNIC copy, 2 passport photos
  • NTS-GAT score card
1
