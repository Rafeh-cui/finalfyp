Departments Offered

  • BS Business Administration (BBA)

Course Registration (FLEX Portal)

  • Log in at flex.comsats.edu.pk
  • Course registration opens 2 weeks before semester start
  • Seek advisor approval for elective courses
  • Maximum 21 credit hours per semester (with dean's permission)

Attendance Policy

Minimum 75% attendance required per course. Students falling below may not be allowed to sit in final exams.

🧠 Mental Health & Wellness

Campus Counseling Services

  • Confidential one-on-one counseling sessions available
  • Group wellness workshops and seminars
  • Academic stress management programs
  • Contact Student Affairs Office to book an appointment

💡 Daily Wellness Tips

  • Sleep 7–8 hours per night for optimal brain function
  • Take 5-minute breaks every 45 minutes of studying
  • Stay hydrated – drink at least 8 glasses of water daily
  • Exercise for 30 minutes at least 3 times a week
  • Practice deep breathing when feeling anxious

💙 You are not alone. Asking for help is a sign of strength.
