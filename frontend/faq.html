🎓 Admissions

What are the admission requirements for BS programs?
Admission to BS programs at COMSATS Attock Campus requires:
  • Intermediate (FSc / ICS / A-Levels) with at least 50% marks
  • Valid NTS / COMSATS Entry Test score
  • Domicile certificate (for merit/seat quota)
  • CNIC and 2 passport-size photographs
  • Original + attested copies of academic documents
When does the admission process open?
COMSATS typically opens admissions twice a year:
  • Spring Semester: January–February
  • Fall Semester: July–August
Check the official COMSATS website or the Attock Campus notice board for exact dates each year.
Are scholarships available for new students?
Yes! Several scholarships are available:
  • HEC Need-Based Scholarship
  • COMSATS Merit Scholarship (top entry test performers)
  • Punjab Educational Endowment Fund (PEEF)
  • Ehsaas Undergraduate Scholarship
Contact the Financial Aid Office for eligibility criteria and application forms.

📖 Academic Queries

How can I get my exam timetable?
Exam timetables are published through:
  • The COMSATS Student Portal (FLEX)
  • Department notice boards
  • Official department WhatsApp groups
  • Email notifications from the Examination Department
Midterm and final exams are typically announced 2 weeks in advance.
What is the grading system at COMSATS?
COMSATS follows a letter grade GPA system:
A+ (95–100) = 4.0A (90–94) = 4.0 A- (85–89) = 3.7B+ (80–84) = 3.3 B (75–79) = 3.0B- (70–74) = 2.7 C+ (65–69) = 2.3C (60–64) = 2.0 D (50–59) = 1.0F (below 50) = 0.0
A CGPA of 2.0 or above is required to remain in good academic standing.
What is the thesis/FYP submission deadline?
Final Year Project (FYP) timelines typically follow:
  • FYP-I (Proposal + Initial Work): End of 7th semester
  • FYP-II (Final Report + Viva): End of 8th semester
  • Exact dates are announced by the department coordinator
  • Late submissions may incur grade penalties
Always confirm with your supervisor and department for semester-specific dates.

🔬 Graduate Programs

What are the MS/PhD admission requirements?
For MS programs:
  • BS degree with minimum 2.5 CGPA (or 45% marks for old system)
  • Valid GRE / NTS-GAT (General) score
  • Statement of purpose and recommendation letters
For PhD:
  • MS/MPhil degree with minimum 3.0 CGPA
  • GRE Subject test score preferred
  • Research proposal and faculty supervisor agreement
How do I find a supervisor for my MS/PhD research?
To find a research supervisor:
  • Browse the CS Department faculty page on the COMSATS website
  • Review faculty research interests and recent publications
  • Email potential supervisors with your research interests and CV
  • Attend departmental seminars to connect with faculty
  • Consult the Graduate Office for guidance on available supervisors

🧠 Mental Health & Wellness

Does COMSATS offer counseling services?
Yes! COMSATS Attock Campus provides:
  • Student Counseling Center – confidential one-on-one sessions
  • Peer support groups and wellness workshops
  • Academic stress management programs
  • Career counseling and guidance services
Contact the Student Affairs Office to schedule an appointment. All sessions are confidential.
How can I manage academic stress effectively?
Here are some tips to manage academic stress:
  • Break large tasks into smaller, manageable steps
  • Use a planner or digital calendar to track deadlines
  • Take regular short breaks using the Pomodoro technique
  • Maintain healthy sleep (7-8 hours), diet, and exercise
  • Talk to friends, family, or a campus counselor when overwhelmed
  • Join study groups for collaborative learning
Remember: it's okay to ask for help! 💙
